You will need LP Tokens from the DEX Pools (BASE) which Zeebu Ecosystem supports

What is LP token:​

LP tokens are digital assets you receive when you deposit your cryptocurrency into a liquidity pool on a decentralized exchange (DEX). These tokens represent your share of the pool and the rewards earned from transaction fees.

For example: If you add $100 worth of tokens to a liquidity pool, you get LP tokens in return. You can redeem them later to withdraw your share, along with any earned rewards. Learn More..

How to become OLP?​

  1. Register on zeebu.fi
  2. Navigate to https://zeebu.fi/stake,
  3. They can see an option to ZBU/USDC Liquidity Pool. (20% ZBU, 80% USDC) Or you can go directly to the balancer pool with same wallet address you used in Zeebu Protocol registration
  4. They can select it and add amount of ZBU and USDC based on the pool value.
  5. They will confirm the transaction in wallet you are using.
  6. They will receive an LP token for that.
  7. They will stake that LP token in platform select the lockin from 6 months, 1 year and 2 year.

Why become an OLP?​

  • Be a Pillar of Decentralization
  • Earn Financial Rewards
  • Actively Shape the Protocols
  • How Does OLP Earn Yield?
  • Support Economic Security: By becoming an OLP, you contribute to the decentralization and security of the Zeebu Protocol, making it more resilient and trustless.
  • Strengthen the Ecosystem: A robust on-chain Liquidity ensures the protocol remains blazing fast, transparent, and resistant to centralization risks.
  • LP Fee Revenue: Earn a share of the transaction fees through your provisioned liquidity, providing a steady stream of APYs.
  • DEX Pool Emissions: Lock LP tokens and harvest additional yield through DEX-native token emissions.
  • Participate in Governance: Help decide protocol upgrades, fee structures, and other critical decisions.
  • Influence Ecosystem Growth: Your involvement drives adoption, scalability, and the overall success of Zeebu.
  • Earn rewards in the form of ZBU tokens and stablecoins.
  • Rewards are proportional to liquidity provided and duration committed.
